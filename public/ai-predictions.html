📊 How the 3-Year Prediction Model Works

Momentum Score

Weights today's price change percentage. Strong positive momentum (>3%) contributes up to 30 pts toward a BUY signal over the long horizon.

Volume Strength

High-volume stocks (5M+ shares) suggest institutional interest. Volume above 1M adds 20 pts; above 500K adds 10 pts to the score.

Checks if current price is above open (bullish intraday) and near day-high (proximity ≥98%). Both factors add up to 30 pts combined.
